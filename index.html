Ice cream is a delicious treat to enjoy, especially in the summer! This recipe will show you step by step on how to create the perfect instant ice cream!

The finished ice cream should look like this!

Ingredients:

  • milk
  • sugar
  • vanilla
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Mix the milk, sugar and vanilla in a bag.
  2. Put the bag in a larger back which contains the salt.
  3. Shake the bags vigorously.
  4. Enjoy!
